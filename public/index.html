My Story

I first stepped into marketing because I was fascinated by how creative storytelling and data analytics together can move people to act. While studying, I was drawn to projects where I could combine strategy with measurable impact, and that curiosity naturally led me into digital marketing.

At OYO Hotels, I managed multi-location campaigns, influencer collaborations, and content calendars that lifted seasonal bookings by 15% and grew Instagram followers by 20% in six months. From there, I expanded into performance marketing at Practo, where I led multi-channel campaigns across Google Ads, LinkedIn, and Email—achieving a 22% increase in qualified leads and reducing cost-per-lead by 18% through analytics-driven optimizations.

Today, I focus on scalable e-commerce and growth marketing: lifecycle campaigns, SEO-led content, and automation that boost conversions and customer LTV. I believe effective marketing is about creating clarity in a noisy digital world — connecting the right audience with genuine value.

Outside of work, I recharge by exploring Vancouver's coffee scene, sketching digital designs, and writing about emerging marketing trends. My goal is to join a team where I can keep solving growth challenges while bringing creative energy and data-driven rigor.
